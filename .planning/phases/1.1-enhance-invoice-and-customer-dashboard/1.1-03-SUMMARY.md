## Decisions Made

1. **No charting library** - Used pure CSS and Tailwind for all visualizations to avoid bundle bloat. CSS conic-gradient and flexbox percentage widths are sufficient for our needs.

2. **Color scheme consistency** - Standardized colors across all pages: green=paid, blue=sent, yellow=pending, red=overdue, gray=draft. Applied to charts, badges, and progress bars.

3. **Days overdue calculation** - Added automatic calculation: `Math.floor((today - dueDate) / (1000 * 60 * 60 * 24))` and displayed as "OVERDUE (X days)" for immediate urgency visibility.

4. **Deferred trend indicators** - Skipped up/down trend arrows because we don't have historical snapshots in the database schema yet. Added TODO comment for future implementation when time-series data becomes available.
