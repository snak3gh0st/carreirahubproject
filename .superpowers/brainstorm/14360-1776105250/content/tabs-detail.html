Financial BI — Tab Contents Detail

Tab 1: Revenue & Growth
Invoiced vs Collected
Area chart — two overlapping series per month. Shows the gap between what was billed and what was actually received.
📊 Type: Area chart (Recharts AreaChart)
📐 X-axis: Month
📏 Y-axis: Dollar amount
🎨 Series: Invoiced (light green fill) + Collected (solid green fill)
Revenue by Service
Horizontal bar chart — breakdown from invoice lineItems JSON. Shows which services generate the most revenue.
📊 Type: Bar chart (horizontal)
📐 X-axis: Dollar amount
📏 Y-axis: Service name
🔢 Source: Parsed from Invoice.lineItems JSON
MRR / ARR Trend
Line chart — monthly recurring revenue calculated from recurring invoices. ARR = MRR x 12 shown as secondary axis or annotation.
📊 Type: Line chart
📐 X-axis: Month
📏 Y-axis: MRR ($)
📎 Note: ARR shown as annotation label
Month-over-Month Growth
Bar chart — percentage growth vs previous month. Green bars for positive, red for negative.
📊 Type: Bar chart (vertical)
📐 X-axis: Month
📏 Y-axis: % change
🎨 Colors: Green (+) / Red (-)
Tab 2: AR & Collections
AR Aging Breakdown
Stacked bar chart + summary table. Five buckets: Current, 1-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+ days.
📊 Type: Stacked bar + data table
🪣 Buckets: Current | 1-30 | 31-60 | 61-90 | 90+
📏 Values: Count + Total $ per bucket
🎨 Colors: Green → Yellow → Orange → Red → Dark Red
Collection Performance
Dual metric: Average days-to-payment trend (line) + collection rate trend (line). Shows if collections are improving over time.
📊 Type: Dual-axis line chart
📐 Left Y: Avg days to payment
📏 Right Y: Collection rate %
📎 Goal: Days ↓ and Rate ↑ = healthy
Overdue Invoices Table
Sortable table of all OVERDUE invoices. Actionable — finance team's primary working list.
📋 Columns: Customer | Invoice # | Amount | Due Date | Days Overdue | Reminders Sent | Collection Calls | Auto-Charge Status | Owner
🔽 Default sort: Days overdue (descending)
🔍 Filter: By owner, by aging bucket
📎 Actions: Click row → opens invoice detail
Tab 3: Cash Flow
Cash Flow Forecast (90 days)
Area chart showing projected inflows based on invoice due dates and customer payment patterns. Three scenarios: optimistic, expected, conservative.
📊 Type: Area chart (3 bands)
📐 X-axis: Date (next 90 days)
📏 Y-axis: Cumulative $ expected
🎨 Bands: Optimistic (light) / Expected (medium) / Conservative (dark)
📎 Source: Existing receivables-forecast API
Payment Probability Breakdown
Donut chart showing outstanding AR split by collection probability. How much of our money is likely vs unlikely to come in?
📊 Type: Donut chart
🥧 Segments: High (>80%) | Medium (50-80%) | Low (20-50%) | Bad debt (<20%)
🎨 Colors: Green | Yellow | Orange | Red
💰 Values: $ amount per segment
At-Risk Invoices
Table of invoices with low collection probability — the ones that need attention NOW.
📋 Columns: Customer | Invoice # | Amount | Due Date | Days Overdue | Probability % | Risk Level | Last Action
🔽 Default sort: Probability ascending (worst first)
🚨 Highlight: CRITICAL risk in red row background
Tab 4: Customer Analysis
Revenue Concentration
Pareto chart — what % of customers generate 80% of revenue? High concentration = business risk.
📊 Type: Combo (bar + cumulative line)
📐 X-axis: Customers (ranked by revenue)
📏 Left Y: Revenue ($)
📏 Right Y: Cumulative %
📎 Insight: "Top 5 clients = X% of revenue"
Top 10 Customers
Horizontal bar — top 10 by total paid amount. Shows who the biggest accounts are.
📊 Type: Horizontal bar chart
📐 X-axis: Revenue ($)
📏 Y-axis: Customer name
📎 Reuse: Existing TopCustomersChart component
Customer Segments
Donut chart — active vs inactive vs churned customers with revenue per segment.
📊 Type: Donut chart
🥧 Segments: Active (invoice in 90d) | Inactive (90-180d) | Churned (>180d)
💰 Inner label: Total revenue per segment
Avg Customer Lifetime Value
KPI card with trend — average total paid per customer. Useful for understanding unit economics.
📊 Type: Large KPI + sparkline trend
💰 Value: Avg total paid per customer
📈 Trend: 6-month rolling average
📎 Secondary: Median LTV (to counter outlier skew)
