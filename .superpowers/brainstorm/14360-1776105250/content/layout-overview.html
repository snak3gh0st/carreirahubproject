Page Structure Summary

  • Header: Title + QB sync status + Date picker + Export buttons (PDF/CSV)

  • Zone 2 — Detailed Tabs:

    • AR & Collections: Aging breakdown table, Overdue invoices list, Collection call outcomes, Days-to-payment
    • Cash Flow: 30/60/90-day forecast, At-risk invoices, Payment probability
    • Customer Analysis: Concentration chart, Top 10 by revenue, Segment breakdown, Churn risk
