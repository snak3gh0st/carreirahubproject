Where AI Adds Real Value
🧠 CFO Briefing Narrative
Instead of template strings, AI writes a natural executive summary
Rule engine feeds AI: revenue=$124.5k (+12%), collection=87% (-5%), overdue=[Acme $8.2k 47d, TechStart $4.1k 31d], concentration=52%

AI generates: "April shows strong top-line growth at $124.5k, but cash conversion is weakening. The 5-point drop in collection rate is concentrated in two accounts — Acme Corp and TechStart — which together represent $12.3k in aging receivables. Given that Acme's auto-charge has failed and TechStart's payment velocity has deteriorated over 3 consecutive months, I'd recommend direct outreach this week. On the positive side, MRR growth of 8% suggests the recurring base is healthy — the issue is collection execution, not demand."
🔮 Strategic Recommendations
AI connects dots across multiple data points
Rule engine feeds AI: concentration=52%, top3=[ClientA 22%, ClientB 18%, ClientC 12%], service_mix=[Mentorship 65%, Resume 20%, Interview 15%], churn_risk=[ClientB payment degrading]

AI generates: "Revenue concentration is your biggest structural risk right now. ClientB's payment behavior suggests possible churn — if they leave, you lose 18% of revenue overnight. Two moves to consider: (1) Launch a referral incentive for mid-tier clients to build the base, (2) Cross-sell Interview Prep to your Mentorship clients — your data shows 0% overlap, which means untapped wallet share."

Cost Control & Caching Strategy
⏰
Daily Cron
AI briefing generated once/day at 8am via Vercel Cron. Cached in DB (new CfoInsight model). ~1 API call/day.
🔄
On-Demand Refresh
"Refresh Analysis" button triggers new AI generation. Rate-limited to 3x/day to control costs.
💰
~$0.15/day
One GPT-4 call with structured financial data = ~3k tokens in, ~500 tokens out. ~$4.50/month total.
Graceful degradation: If OpenAI is down or key is missing, the dashboard shows all rule-based data normally. AI sections show "Analysis updating..." with last cached version timestamp. Never blocks the dashboard.
