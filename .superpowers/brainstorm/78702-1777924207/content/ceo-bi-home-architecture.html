Home executiva do BI unico

Estrutura recomendada para o cockpit CEO-first com home unica e aprofundamento por area.

Executive Flight Deck: recommended home architecture
1. CEO Briefing Hero
One-screen read: what changed, what matters, what decision is needed
2. Decision Queue
3-5 urgent items ranked by business impact
Cash on hand
Net margin
Revenue pace
AR at risk
Runway / burn
3. Business Health Story
Cash, margin, collections and trend context in one narrative block
4. Risk Map
Clients, invoices, operations or AI issues threatening the business
Finance area entry
Sales area entry
Ops area entry
AI area entry
5. Cross-area performance canvas
Revenue, pipeline, delivery, customer outcomes in one comparison view
6. Momentum and watchlist
What is improving, what is stalling, what needs follow-up this week

Why this order

Best-practice rationale

  • The first fold answers the CEO's first question: are we healthy, and what decision is waiting?
  • The KPI strip is short and non-duplicative: only metrics that express business health at a glance.
  • Risk comes before area drill-down, so the user sees what can hurt the company before exploring details.
  • Area entries appear only after the executive picture is clear, preserving hierarchy.

Guardrails

  • No raw wall of charts at the top.
  • No duplicate KPI values repeated in finance, sales and ops blocks.
  • No area tabs competing with the CEO briefing hero.
  • Each area must inherit the same executive framing when drilled into.
